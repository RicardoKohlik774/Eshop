You can interact with this program by typing the avaible command in the console. Such as:
Browse -----> Displays the current items and their name, stock, and price.
Buy --------> You can add products to your cart.
Remove -----> You can remove products from your cart.
Checkout ---> Asks you to confirm the items in the order, and after inputting valid info, it creates an order that the admin has to confirm and clears your cart.
Stash ------> Here you can check your current owned items after they arrive.
Inbox ------> You can check the status of your current orders here.
